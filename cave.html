Crystal Cave

Welcome to Crystal Cave. This is possibly the most magical place on Treasure Island. It may also be the most dangerous. Let's see how you do.

Instructions:

  1. To collect specimens, click on their images below.
  2. As you gather your specimen samples, you will see your accumulated risk increase. each specimen provides a different level of risk. As you gather specimens, pay close attention to your accumulated risk!
  3. If your accumulated risk reaches the risk threshold without going over, you may leave the cave safely with your collection.
  4. However, if your accumulated risk surpasses the risk threshold, you won't survive Crystal Cave. Best of luck to you!
