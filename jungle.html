Emerald Jungle

Welcome to the jungle. This place is absolutely teeming with undiscovered wonders. If you're careful, you will add some incredible specimens to your collection. And if you're not careful...

Instructions:

  1. To collect specimens, click on their images below.
  2. As you gather your specimen samples, you will see your accumulated risk increase. each specimen provides a different level of risk. As you gather specimens, pay close attention to your accumulated risk!
  3. If your accumulated risk reaches the risk threshold without going over, you may leave the jungle safely with your collection.
  4. However, if your accumulated risk surpasses the risk threshold, you won't survive this mission. Best of luck to you!
